Original Text

Kyrshaxis, the exalted one, divine shepherd of man, king of kings, do hereby decree: the state of Hejkos shall be punished for the part it played in the war. Each year on the summer solstice Hejkos shall tithe one-eighth of its gold, a great gross bushels of grain or drink, and two regiments of trained men for use in Kyrshaxis' military. Failure to deliver in a timely manner shall result in the lands being ransacked and reparations increased.
