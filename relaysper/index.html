Original Text

Kyrshaxis, the exalted one, divine shepherd of man, king of kings, do hereby decree: the state of Hejkos shall be punished for the part it played in the war. Each year on the summer solstice Hejkos shall tithe one-eighth of its gold, a great gross bushels of grain or drink, and two regiments of trained men for use in Kyrshaxis' military. Failure to deliver in a timely manner shall result in the lands being ransacked and reparations increased.

Translation

King Kirshasi, high-priest, lord of lords, (it is hoped that) this may be seen and listened to. Due to decalartion of war, chief Hikhosa was sentenced to punishment, thus it is known. On to the Solstice, sacrifices are brought those (being) donated (are) an eight of Hikosa's gold, many vessals of beer or grain as well as a troop trained by the warriors of Kirshas. Those who heed not (this call) will be sent into the mountains and be offered up in sacrifice, (thus so may) this be known.
