King Kirshasi, high-priest, lord of lords, (it is hoped that) this may be seen and listened to.
Due to decalartion of war, chief Hikhosa was sentenced to punishment, thus it is known.

many vessals of beer or grain as well as a troop trained by the warriors of Kirshas
Those who heed not (this call) will be sent into the mountains and be offered up in sacrifice, (thus so may) this be known.
