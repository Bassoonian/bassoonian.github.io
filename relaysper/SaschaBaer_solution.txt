Praise Gile·sagisi! He, who teaches his citizens in the sayings of God, the king of kings, announces this important decision:
The Kingdom of Haekasa will be punished using this schedule. Haekasa must pay one eight (lit. 1½ twelfths) of their gold, a lot of beer and grain and two battalions, which will be lead by Gile·sagisi’s soldiers in many fights in future years. The products will be delivered before that time or the kingdom will be pillaged and demands increased.
