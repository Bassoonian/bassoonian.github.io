Some notes
==========
*this text has two names: kelehaykeses [ˈke.le.haj.ke.ses] ekuasa [e.ˈkʷa.sa] 
*proto ãrãnee is a nominative-accusative langauge has 5 gramatical cases, Nominative, Accusative, Vocative, Genitive and Instrumental.
*
 
Grammar
==========

Gramatical cases 
		Singular	Plural
Nominative 	Ø		Ø
Accusative	-óf		-ew
Genitive	-áh		-áł	
Vocative	-se		-uk
Instrumental	-uy		-ut

*when the word end with a consonant case marking with the thematic vowel is allowed, but when the word end witha a vowel the thematic vowel is not allowed.

Articles
		Masc	Fem	Indf.
1st		ay	łu	oʔ
2nd		ów 	uy

*"oʔ" is both 1st and 2nd article
