Phonotactics

||PHONOTACTICS||

Suprasegmentals

Stress fell on the penultimate of the stem - as such, prefixes were never stressed.

Morphophonology

Besides an active regressive voicing assimilation filter, we may find the collapse of two identical vowels into a single long vowel of the same quality, i.e. |V₁V₁(ː)| /V₁ː/. Intervocalic |b d g| always surfaced as /β ð ɣ/.
