Suprasegmentals

The analysis of stress varies, but the one presented here is the most common. Stress is always penultimate, unless the antepenultimate syllable has a coda, in which case that syllable receives stress instead. Apparent violations can easily be explained through the presence of an additional empty syllable, which causes final or, per the rules above, potentially penultimate stress instead.
