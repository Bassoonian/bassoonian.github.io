List of Holidays

Notice: This page is still under construction. Information presented here is subject to be developed upon or to be scrapped entirely.

This page lists all days of particular importance to the Carites at all points throughout history. As such, some may no longer be observed or have only come into existence relatively recently. The list is ordered chronologically per year.

GJAMONJOS

Originally a month name of Gaulish origin, this word originally referred to the 7th month of the Celtic calendar, usually equated to December or January. With the adoption of the Roman Calendar, it remained in use to refer to a pagan holiday celebrated on the 1st of January.
